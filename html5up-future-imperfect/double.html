Cathedral Rock Trail- Arizona

Moderate Hike Located in the Coconino National Forest

January 04, 2020 Madalina Marian

This heavily trafficked 1.2-mile hike is located in Sedona, Arizona. The hike only took me about an hour roundtrip, but it could get pretty steep at times but not impossible. More than half of the trail is out in the sunlight so make sure to apply some sunscreen and have lots of water to hydrate yourself with. I usually bring a snack and enjoy it as I get to the top that way I have something to look forward to. Once you reach the "end" sign, take a left and climb up for about another minute or so to see a second view. That way you get to see not one but two beautiful sceneries and you won't be disappointed.
