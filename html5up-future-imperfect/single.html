Cabo Da Roca

The most westerly point of mainland Europe

May 14, 2019 Madalina Marian

This is by far most the most beautiful hike I have ever been on. Although, it was a very short one; about a 10-minute walk to the edge. It's located in Colares, Portugal...about a thirty-five-minute drive from Lisbon, the capital. It could get a bit windy so be careful when looking over the edge especially since many tourists have been known to fall over the edge when trying to get a good shot. The fresh breeze and the sound of the crashing waves was very relaxing. And on top of that, there's also a very beautiful scenery.

If Portugal is on your bucket-list, this is a place you must visit. These cliffs were known to be the "edge of the world" up until the 14th century. But as of now, standing on the edge means your standing on the most westerly point of mainland Europe.
